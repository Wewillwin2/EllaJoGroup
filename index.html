Privacy Policy

Your privacy is
our commitment.

Ella Jo Group is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of every individual who engages with our website or our services. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. Last updated: 2025.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to respond to your inquiry and provide our services. This may include:

  • Name, email address, and company or organization name submitted through our contact form
  • General website usage data collected via Google Analytics (anonymized and aggregated)
  • Any additional information you voluntarily provide during a consultation or ongoing engagement

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used solely to respond to your inquiry, schedule consultations, and deliver our services. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

Confidentiality

Discretion is central to everything we do. All client information — including the nature of our engagement, the details of your business, and any personal information shared — is treated with the highest level of confidentiality. Our team members are bound by strict confidentiality standards at all times.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand general visitor behavior and improve user experience. This data is anonymized and does not identify individual users. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking at any time by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we encourage you to exercise caution when sharing sensitive information online.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you, to request corrections, or to request that we delete your information. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@ellajogroup.com.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how your information is handled, please reach out to us directly at hello@ellajogroup.com. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
